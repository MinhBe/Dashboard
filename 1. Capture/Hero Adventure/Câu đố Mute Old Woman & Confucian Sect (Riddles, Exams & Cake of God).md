## Cake of God Achievement

1. Set PC date to **Dragon Boat Festival** (June 22 or May 31)
2. Go to **Chuxiang tavern**, answer 5 questions
3. Then go to **Lin'an pier**
4. Answer questions correctly to receive **Cake of God**

*(Full question list available in the complete guide.)*

## Top Achiever Trait

1. Get **2 top examiner** traits
2. Introduce as scholar when saving the Emperor
3. Fight 4 generals with poetry lines
